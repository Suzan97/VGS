About Us

Initially begins with Kenya, East Africa then the entire Africa at large.

Get to know about our company

Ventures GrandStage (VGS) is a platform for entrepreneurs in Africa, with a mandate of showcasing some of the best commercial ventures in business within Africa to meet investors.

The platform will focus on unique and viable business and trade opportunities that have great growth potential to invest in.

Read More
More about us
The goal is to encourage entrepreneurship in already existing business ventures from around the continent to be noticed by potential investors worldwide & globally.

We will analyze their money making potential, and explain and evaluate the risks and rewards of each business opportunity highlighted on the platform. This will be an Entrepreneurs mentorship and capacity building center.

We will aim to improve economic development in the region by supporting entrepreneurs at all level of their businesses from start-up, micro enterprises and growing SMEs with skills, mentorship, essential services, networking and where possible finance arrangement with willing investors.
